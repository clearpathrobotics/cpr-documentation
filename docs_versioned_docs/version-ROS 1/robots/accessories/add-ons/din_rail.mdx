### Instructions

1.  Mount the DIN Rail (ID 2) to the attachment-plate (ID 1) using the M5 X 10 screws (ID 3) and nuts (ID 4).
2.  Mount this assembly onto your robot, using the included spacers (ID 5) and screws (ID 6).
