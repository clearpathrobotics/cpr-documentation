### Instructions

1.  Mount the sensor (ID 2) to the attachment-plate (ID 1) using two M3 screws (ID 3).
    Refer to the [attachment-plate's drawing](/assets/pdf/clearpath_robotics_026914.pdf) for details of what holes to use.
2.  Mount this assembly onto your robot, using the included spacers (ID 4) and screws (ID 5).
3.  Connect the sensor's RJ45 network cable to the robot's computer or network switch.
4.  Add a connector to the sensor's power cable, so it can interface with your robot's User Power breakouts.
    Ensure that the cable pinout is correct, and can supply 12 V or 24 V to the sensor.
5.  Connect the sensor's power cable to the robot's User Power breakouts.
